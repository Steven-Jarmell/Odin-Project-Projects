Smash Burgers Recipe

Description

Quick and easy to cook smash burgers

Ingredients

  • 16 oz 80/20 ground beef
  • Multiple slices of a cheese of your preference
  • Burger buns
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Canola Oil

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into .25lb balls and cover with salt and pepper
  2. Pour canola oil into a cast iron or pan and let it get ripping hot
  3. Place one of the balls into the pan and smash it down as flat as you can (You should hear it sizzle immediately
  4. Cook for about a minute then flip
  5. Add a slice of cheese, put a few drops of water in the pan, cover, and turn off the heat
  6. Wait about 20 seconds for the cheese to melt and enjoy!
