Smash Burgers Recipe

Description

Quick and easy to cook smash burgers

Ingredients

  • 16 oz 80/20 ground beef
  • Multiple slices of a cheese of your preference
  • Burger buns
  • Salt/Pepper
  • Canola Oil

Steps

  • Form ground beef into .25lb balls and cover with salt and pepper
  • Pour canola oil into a cast iron or pan and let it get ripping hot
  • Place one of the balls into the pan and smash it down as flat as you can (You should hear it sizzle immediately
  • Cook for about a minute then flip
  • Add a slice of cheese, put a few drops of water in the pan, cover, and turn off the heat
  • Wait about 20 seconds for the cheese to melt and enjoy!
